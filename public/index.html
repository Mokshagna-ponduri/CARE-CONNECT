About CareConnect

CareConnect is a community-driven platform that bridges the gap between people who need help and those willing to offer it. In times of personal or public crisis, people can easily reach out for support or volunteer to help others, making communities resilient and caring.

Our Mission

To create stronger, more connected communities by facilitating meaningful help exchanges between neighbors.

How It Works

  1. Create a Request: Post what you need help with, from food delivery to tutoring.
  2. Find Help: Browse nearby requests and offer your assistance.
  3. Connect: Chat securely with other community members.
  4. Help & Rate: Complete the help exchange and leave feedback.

Help Center

Getting Started

  • How to create an account: Click "Register" and fill in your details
  • How to post a help request: Click "Create Request" and describe what you need
  • How to offer help: Browse requests and click "Offer Help" on ones you can assist with
  • How to chat with others: Use the chat feature once you've connected with someone

Safety & Security

  • Meeting safely: Always meet in public places
  • Verifying users: Look for verified badges on user profiles
  • Reporting issues: Use the report button if you encounter problems
  • Privacy settings: Control what information is visible on your profile

Account & Settings

  • Updating profile: Go to Profile section to edit your information
  • Changing password: Use the Profile section to update your password
  • Notification settings: Manage your email and app notifications
  • Deleting account: Contact support to request account deletion

Safety Guidelines

Verify Before Meeting

Always verify the identity of the person you're meeting. Look for verified badges and read their profile reviews.

Meet in Public Places

Choose well-lit, public locations for meetings. Coffee shops, libraries, and community centers are good options.

Stay Connected

Let a friend or family member know where you're going and who you're meeting. Keep your phone charged and accessible.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, don't proceed. It's okay to cancel or reschedule if you're uncomfortable.

Report Concerns

If you encounter suspicious behavior or feel unsafe, report it immediately using our reporting system.
